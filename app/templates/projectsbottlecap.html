For three years, I collected the bottle caps from all the Michigan beers I consumed with the intent of eventually building a map of Michigan with them. When the U.S. government was "shut down" in October 2013, I knew it was the perfect opportunity.

This imgur slideshow documents the process, from laying it all out in Google Sketchup, to preparing the wood, and ultimately gluing on hundreds of bottle caps. It was a fun project, and hopefully will be with me for some time.
